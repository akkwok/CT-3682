Flexible & Powerful Cooling
Your Choice of Hybrid or 100% Liquid Cooling

Addressing the most rigorous demands of the modern data center, ASUS proudly introduces the XA NR1I-E12L server series built on the NVIDIA® HGX™ Rubin platform. To ensure a seamless and cost-effective transition to liquid cooling, ASUS offers two distinct solutions tailored to your infrastructure needs.

XA NR1I-E12L

Hybrid Cooled

This innovative design combines direct-to-chip (D2C) liquid cooling for the high-performance NVIDIA HGX Rubin 8-GPU baseboard with efficient air cooling for the dual Intel® Xeon® 6 processors. It offers a perfect balance of performance and ease of adoption.

XA NR1I-E12LR

Fully liquid-Cooled

Designed for maximum thermal efficiency and density, this 100% liquid-cooled system is the ultimate solution for handling the most intensive AI workloads in high-density environments.

FAQ

  • What is the difference between hybrid and fully liquid-cooled HGX Rubin servers?
    What is the difference between hybrid and fully liquid-cooled HGX Rubin servers? • XA NR1I-E12L (Hybrid-Cooled): Combines liquid cooling for the NVIDIA HGX 8-GPU baseboard with air cooling for the CPUs. It's ideal for an easy transition to liquid cooling.
    • XA NR1I-E12LR (Fully Liquid-Cooled): Provides 100% liquid cooling for maximum thermal performance and density in demanding data centers.
  • What are the main benefits of liquid cooling for an NVIDIA HGX Rubin 8-GPU AI server?
    What are the main benefits of liquid cooling for an NVIDIA HGX Rubin 8-GPU AI server? Liquid cooling is essential for next-generation AI servers. Key benefits include:
    • Peak Performance: Prevents thermal throttling of powerful GPUs.
    • Higher Density: Allows more servers per rack, maximizing compute power.
    • Greater Efficiency: Lowers data center energy consumption and operational costs.
  • How does the NVIDIA HGX Rubin platform enhance AI performance?
    How does the NVIDIA HGX Rubin platform enhance AI performance? The NVIDIA HGX Rubin platform accelerates AI workloads by providing:
    • Extreme Power: Manages the high thermal demands of the latest GPUs.
    • Massive Bandwidth: Offers 800G of integrated bandwidth per GPU to eliminate data bottlenecks.
    • High Availability: Features a modular sled design for faster service and maximum uptime.
  • What is the reference architecture of ASUS AI POD with NVIDIA HGX Rubin platform?
    What is the reference architecture of ASUS AI POD with NVIDIA HGX Rubin platform? The ASUS AI Factory is a pre-validated, rack-scale blueprint that integrates multiple ASUS HGX Rubin servers with all necessary networking, power, and storage. It provides a proven path to rapidly deploy a complete AI supercomputer, scaling from a single server to a full rack with confidence.
  • How does the server’s modular design improve serviceability and reduce downtime?
    How does the server’s modular design improve serviceability and reduce downtime? The server features a modular design with tool-less access to critical components like the CPU and GPU sleds. This allows for significantly faster maintenance and upgrades, which directly reduces operational downtime and lowers the total cost of ownership (TCO).
